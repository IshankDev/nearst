Cancellation Policy:


Any product ordered on our app can be cancelled through the app until the product has been delivered to you by us. This is applicable for all products. However, on frequent cancellations, the said customer might be marked by our systems as frauds and service would be clocked for the said user. Total order amount will be refunded to your bank account.
To modify your shipping address, you will be required to call our customer support/email us about the same and we would do the best we can to help you.
